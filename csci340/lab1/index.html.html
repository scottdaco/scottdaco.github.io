Snail Crate Volleyball

Very fun snail activity

Snail Instructions

Before you begin this activity you must gather these three things.

Snails

You need a bunch of snails. These are located in places like:

  • outside
  • under a rock

Crate

You need a crate to put the snails in. You can find these at:

  • The crate store

VolleyBall

The last thing you need is a volley ball. You can find these at:

  • a store
  • make one
